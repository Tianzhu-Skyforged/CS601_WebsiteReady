Beautiful British Columbia

Victoria and Kamloops are two Canadian cities that I have lived in. Both of them are located in Birtish Colombia, Canada's westernmost province. I spent about six years in total over there studying as an international student.(I am still one hehe) I want to honor them here since I had a lot of good memories there and they deserve to be known by more people.

Kamloops.BC

Kamloops is a city in south-central British Columbia, Canada, at the confluence of the two branches of the Thompson River and east of Kamloops Lake. Kamloops is known as the Tournament Capital of Canada. It hosts more than 100 tournaments each year at world-class sports facilities such as the Tournament Capital Centre, Kamloops Bike Ranch, and Tournament Capital Ranch. Source:https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kamloops

Top things to do in Kamloops:

  • Skiing!!!
  • Mountain bike riding
  • Try some great wine at local Wineries
  • Try a gem brunch place called: hello toast
  • Enjoy the "beach" at riverside park
